📘 Introduction

This guide describes the procedures for accessing FTC HUB, completing the first login, and managing your user profile, including password changes.

🌐 Application URL: https://hub.nexturelab.com
🔑 1. System Access

This section describes the procedure to access FTC HUB.

  1. Open your browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox) and navigate to https://hub.nexturelab.com
  2. On the login screen, enter your Username in the appropriate field (see the table below for the format)
  3. Enter the Password you received
  4. Click the Login button
  5. If the credentials are correct, you will be redirected to the main Dashboard
📸 Screenshot: FTC HUB login screen with username and password fields

⚠️ In case of error: If the message "Invalid credentials" appears, verify that you have entered your username and password correctly. After multiple failed attempts, contact the system administrator.
✅ Persistent session: The session remains active — you do not need to log in on every visit.
🆕 2. First Login

What to do on your first access to the system.

  1. On your first login, use the temporary password received by email or provided by your manager
  2. After logging in, immediately change your password from the Profile section (see Section 3)
  3. Choose a secure and personal password
🔒 Important: It is recommended to use a password different from the one used to access other company services.
👤 3. Profile Management

How to access and edit your personal data and password.

3.1 — Access Your Profile

  1. In the left sidebar, locate your username displayed at the bottom
  2. Click on your name to open the Profile page
📸 Screenshot: sidebar with the username highlighted at the bottom left

3.2 — Edit Personal Data

  1. In the "Personal data" section, you can edit: Username, Email, Phone
  2. Make the desired changes in the editable fields
  3. Click the Save changes button to confirm
  4. Changes are immediate and active right away
📸 Screenshot: "Personal data" section with Username, Email, Phone fields and Save changes button

3.3 — Change Password

  1. In the "Change password" section, enter your Current password
  2. Enter the New password of your choice
  3. Re-enter the new password in the Confirm password field
  4. Click the Update password button
  5. The new password is active immediately: use it from your next login onward
📸 Screenshot: "Change password" section with current password, new password, confirm, and "Update password" button
💡 Tip: For a secure password, use at least 8 characters, including uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and a special character (e.g. ! @ #).
